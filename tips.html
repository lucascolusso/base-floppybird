Welcome to the Flappy Bird game!
Your performance will be compared to past players' scores.

We hope you have fun while guiding our friend Flappy Bird through the pipes
without crashing into them or letting the Flappy Bird fall on the ground.

Game controls

1. In the next screen, press "Spacebar" on your keyboard to start the first round.




"Spacebar" is also the key you need to press to make Flappy Bird fly higher (i.e., flap wings).

2. After each round, you'll see a popup with information about your performace.

In the popup, you will also have the option to play a new round or exit the game.
To play a new round, click the "Play again" button.
To exit the game, click the "Exit game" button.
